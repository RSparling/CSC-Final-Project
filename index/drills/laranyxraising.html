Big Dog/Small Dog

The big dog/small dog exercise is based around exploiting a tendancy on how a person often tries to make higher pitch sounds through a combination of raising their laraynx in conjuction to tightening their vocal folds.

How to Perform

To practice the Big Down/Small Dog exercise first start with making a pant like that of a dog. It should be a breathy sound with very little vocalization to it and at roughly the same pitch as normally speak. Do this twice in quick succession followed by a higher third pant. When doing this you should feel your laranxy raise and the muscle connected to it tigthen. Once you feel that you cant get it as high as you can, you then move onto the vocalization portion.

Vocalization

To preform the vocalization test and pracitce, simply hold a higher picture note and then move into speaking short passage or singing a few lyrics from a song that you wish to use to practice this quality.
